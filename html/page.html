The Kingdom of Saudi Arabia is an Arab country and is considered one of the largest countries in the Middle East, with an area of ​​about 2 million square kilometers. The country's land borders are Jordan, Iraq, Kuwait, Qatar, Oman, Yemen and the Emirates, and the maritime borders are Kuwait, Bahrain, Iran, Qatar, the Emirates, Jordan, Egypt, Sudan and Yemen.
